Aglio e olio

Aglio e Olio Pasta, also known as Garlic and Olive Oil, is a traditional pasta dish made with simple and fresh flavors. It truly is more than the sum of its parts.

Recipes

Ingredients

  • 150 grams of Pasta
  • Water
  • 2 tbsp of Salt
  • Chicken
  • 3 cloves of Garlics
  • 2-3 chillies
  • Oregano
  • 2 spoons of Oil

Steps

1

Pour enough water into the pot, then turn on the stove. Next, add a little salt and wait until it boils. while waiting for the water to boil, we can cut the chicken into cubes.

2


After that, we can  stir-fry the chicken by adding the oil first. Next, Put the salt and pepper. And then lift.

3

When the water is already boiling, now let's add the pasta and wait untill it boils.

4

Next, Make the Sauce= Make sure the pan is cold. Pour about 5-6 tablespoons of olive oil along with the onions and chilies. Turn on the fire, cook on low heat until fragrant. Add salt, black pepper, tomatoes and stir briefly. Turn off the stove

5

After that, when The pasta is already boiling, we pour the water into the sink and then pour the pasta into the sauce 

6

Next, Pour in the oregano and chicken. Stir until each piece of pasta is coated in the sauce. Finally, pour into a plate, and..ready to eat
